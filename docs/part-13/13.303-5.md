# 13.303-5 Purchases under BPAs

(a) Use a BPA only for purchases that are otherwise authorized by law or regulation.

(b) Individual purchases shall  not exceed the simplified acquisition threshold . However, agency

regulations may establish a higher threshold consistent with the following:

(1) The simplified acquisition threshold  and the $9 million  limitation for individual purchases ($15

million for purchases entered into under the authority of 12.102 (f)(1)) do not apply to BPAs

established in accordance with 13.303-2 (c)(3).
