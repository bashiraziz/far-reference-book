# 13.001 Definitions

As used in this part-

Authorized individual  means a person who has been granted authority, in accordance with agency

procedures, to acquire supplies  and services in accordance with this part.

Governmentwide commercial purchase card  means a purchase card, similar in nature to a

commercial credit card, issued to authorized agency personnel to use to acquire and to pay for

supplies  and services.

Imprest fund  means a cash fund of a fixed amount established by an advance of funds, without

charge to an appropriation, from an agency finance or disbursing officer to a duly appointed cashier,

for disbursement as needed from time to time in making payment in cash for relatively small

amounts.

Third party draft  means an agency bank draft, similar to a check, that is used to acquire and to pay

for supplies  and services. (See Treasury Financial Management Manual, Section 3040.70.)
