# 13.404 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.213-1](/part-52/52.213-1) , Fast Payment Procedure, in solicitations

and contracts when the conditions in [13.402](/part-13/13.402)  are applicable and it is intended that the fast payment

procedure be used in the contract (in the case of BPAs, the contracting officer  may elect to insert the

clause either in the BPA or in orders under the BPA).
