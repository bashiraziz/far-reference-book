# 13.305-1 General

Imprest funds  and third party drafts  may be used to acquire and to pay for supplies  or services.

Policies and regulations concerning the establishment of and accounting for imprest funds  and third

party drafts , including the responsibilities of designated cashiers and alternates , are contained in

PartIV of the Treasury Financial Manual for Guidance of Departments and Agencies, Title 7 of the

GAO Policy and Procedures Manual for Guidance of Federal Agencies , and the agency implementing

regulations. Agencies also shall  be guided by the Manual of Procedures and Instructions for

Cashiers, issued by the Financial Management Service, Department of the Treasury.
