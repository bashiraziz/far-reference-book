# 30.000 Scope of part

This part describes policies and procedures for applying the Cost Accounting Standards Board

(CASB) rules and regulations ( 48 CFR Chapter 99 ) to negotiated contracts and subcontracts. This

part does not apply to sealed bid contracts or to any contract with a small business concern (see 48

CFR 9903.201-1(b) for these and other exemptions).
