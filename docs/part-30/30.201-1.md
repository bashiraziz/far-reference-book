# 30.201-1 CAS applicability

(a) See 48 CFR 9903.201-1 .

(b) In accordance with 41 U.S.C. 1502 (b)(1)(B), the threshold for determining the tentative

applicability of CAS at the contract level is the amount set forth in 10 U.S.C. 3702(a)(1)(A) , as

adjusted for inflation in accordance with 41 U.S.C. 1908 .
