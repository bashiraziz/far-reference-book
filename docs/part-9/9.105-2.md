# 9.105-2 (b)(2)(iv)

(g) Pre-notice letter . Prior to initiating a suspension , a pre-notice letter may be issued at the

discretion of the agency suspending and debarring official . A pre-notice letter is not required to

initiate suspension  under this subpart. (See [9.403](/part-9/9.403) .)
