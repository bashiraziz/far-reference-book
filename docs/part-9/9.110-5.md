# 9.110-5 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.209-14](/part-52/52.209-14) , Reserve Officer Training Corps and

Military Recruiting on Campus, in solicitations  and contracts that are expected to exceed the

simplified acquisition threshold , with institutions of higher education, when using funds from a

covered agency . The clause is not prescribed for solicitations  and contracts using part  12  for the

acquisition  of commercial products  and commercial services .
