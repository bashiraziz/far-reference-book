# 9.109-3 Exception

The prohibition in [9.109-2](/part-9/9.109-2)  does not apply to contracts for the procurement  of products  or services

along a major route of supply to a zone of active combat or major contingency operation , as specified

in statute or by the cognizant Combatant Commander , in consultation with the Chief of Mission . As

of May 10, 2018, countries along the major route of supply to support operations in Afghanistan are

Afghanistan, Georgia, the Kyrgyz Republic, Pakistan, the Republic of Armenia, the Republic of

Azerbaijan, the Republic of Kazakhstan, the Republic of Tajikistan, the Republic of Uzbekistan, and

Turkmenistan.
