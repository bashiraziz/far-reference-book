# 9.109-5 Solicitation provision

Unless the exception at [9.109-3](/part-9/9.109-3)  applies, the contracting officer  shall  include the provision at

[52.209-13](/part-52/52.209-13) , Violation of Arms Control Treaties or Agreements-Certification, in each solicitation  for

the acquisition  of products  or services (including construction ) that exceeds the simplified

acquisition threshold , other than solicitations  for the acquisition  of commercial products  or

commercial services .
