# 9.104-6 Federal Awardee Performance and Integrity Information System

(a)

(1) Before awarding a contract in excess of the simplified acquisition threshold , the contracting

officer  shall  review the performance and integrity information available in the Federal Awardee

Performance and Integrity Information System (FAPIIS), (available at https://www.cpars.gov ),

including FAPIIS information from the System for Award Management (SAM ) Exclusions and the

Contractor Performance Assessment Reporting System (CPARS).

(see 52.204-20 , Predecessor of Offeror ).

(b)

(1) When making a responsibility determination, the contracting officer  shall  consider all the

information available through FAPIIS with regard to the offeror  and any immediate owner,

predecessor, or subsidiary identified for that offeror  in FAPIIS, as well as other past performance

information on the offeror  (see subpart  42.15 ).
