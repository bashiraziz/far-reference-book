# 6.000 Scope of part

This part prescribes policies and procedures to promote full and open competition  in the acquisition

process and to provide for full and open competition , full and open competition  after exclusion of

sources, other than full and open competition , and advocates for competition. This part does not deal

with the results of competition ( e.g., adequate price competition), that are addressed in other parts

(e.g., part  15 ).
