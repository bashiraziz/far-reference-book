# 6.204 Section 8(a) competition

(a) To fulfill statutory requirements relating to section 8(a) of the Small Business Act, as amended by

Public Law 100-656, contracting officers  may limit competition to eligible 8(a) participants (see

subpart  [19.8](/part-19/19.8) ).

(b) No separate justification or determination and findings is required under this part to limit

competition to eligible 8(a) participants. (But see [6.302-5](/part-6/6.302-5)  and [6.303-1](/part-6/6.303-1)  for sole source 8(a) awards

over $30 million .)
