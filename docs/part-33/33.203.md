# 33.203 (b) apply. If it is determined under agency procedures that continued performance is

necessary pending resolution of any claim  arising under or relating to the contract, the contracting

officer  shall  use the clause with its AlternateI.

(b) Insert the clause at [52.233-4](/part-52/52.233-4)  in all solicitations  and contracts.
