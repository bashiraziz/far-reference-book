# 33.210 Contracting officer’s authority

Except as provided in this section, contracting officers  are authorized, within any specific limitations

of their warrants, to decide or resolve all claims  arising under or relating to a contract subject to the

Disputes statute. In accordance with agency policies and [33.214](/part-33/33.214) , contracting officers  are authorized

to use ADR procedures to resolve claims . The authority to decide or resolve claims  does not extend

to-

(a) A claim  or dispute for penalties or forfeitures prescribed by statute or regulation that another

Federal agency  is specifically authorized to administer, settle, or determine; or

(b) The settlement, compromise, payment, or adjustment of any claim  involving fraud.
