# 5.001 Definition

Contract action , as used in this part, means an action resulting in a contract, as defined in subpart

[2.1](/part-2/2.1), including actions for additional supplies  or services outside the existing contract scope, but not

including actions that are within the scope and under the terms of the existing contract, such as

contract modifications  issued pursuant to the Changes clause, or funding and other administrative

changes.
