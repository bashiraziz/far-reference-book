# 5.002 Policy

Contracting officers  must  publicize contract actions  in order to-

(a) Increase competition;

(b) Broaden industry participation in meeting Government requirements; and

(c) Assist small business concerns, veteran-owned small business concerns, service-disabled veteran-

owned small business concerns, HUBZone  small business concerns, small disadvantaged business

concerns , and women-owned small business concerns  in obtaining contracts and subcontracts.
