# 5.204 Presolicitation notices

Contracting officers  must  provide access to presolicitation notices through the GPE (see [15.201](/part-15/15.201)  and

[36.213-2](/part-36/36.213-2) ). The contracting officer  must  synopsize a proposed contract action  before issuing any

resulting solicitation  (see [5.201](/part-5/5.201)  and [5.203](/part-5/5.203) ).
