# 27.204-1 Use of patented technology under the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement

When questions arise with regard to use of patented technology under the United States -Mexico-

Canada Agreement, the contracting officer  should  consult with legal counsel. Note that Article

[20.6](/part-20/20.6)(a) of the Agreement discusses public health and pharmaceuticals.
