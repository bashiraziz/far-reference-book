# 27.203-2 Contract clause

Insert the clause at 52.227-10 , Filing of Patent Applications-Classified Subject Matter, in all

classified solicitations  and contracts and in all solicitations  and contracts where the nature of the

work reasonably might result in a patent application containing classified subject matter.
