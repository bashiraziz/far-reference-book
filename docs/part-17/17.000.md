# 17.000 Scope of part

This part prescribes policies and procedures for the acquisition  of supplies  and services through

special contracting  methods, including-

(a) Multi-year contracting ;

(b) Options ;

(c) Leader company contracting ; and

(d) The use of reverse auctions  to obtain competitive pricing .
