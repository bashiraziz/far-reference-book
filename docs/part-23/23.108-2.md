# 23.108-2 Chemically-intensive products

(a) Program.  Safer Choice is EPA's label for products  that contain safer chemicals. Every chemical,

regardless of percentage, in a Safer Choice-certified product is evaluated through EPA's rigorous

scientific process and only the safest ingredients are allowed.

(b) Resource.  For information on Safer Choice-certified products , see https://www.epa.gov/

saferchoice .
