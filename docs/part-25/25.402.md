# 25.402 (b)). Agencies other than the Department of Defense, the Department of Energy, the

Department of Transportation, the Bureau of Reclamation of the Department of the Interior, the

Federal Housing Finance Board, and the Office of Thrift Supervision must  evaluate offers  of Israeli

end products  without regard to the restrictions of the Buy American statute. The Israeli Trade Act

does not prohibit the purchase of other foreign end products .
