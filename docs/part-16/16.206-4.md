# 16.206-4 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall , when contracting  by negotiation, insert the clause at [52.216-6](/part-52/52.216-6) , Price

Redetermination-Retroactive, in solicitations  and contracts when a fixed-price contract is

contemplated and the conditions in [16.206-2](/part-16/16.206-2)  and [16.206-3](/part-16/16.206-3) (a) through (d) apply.
