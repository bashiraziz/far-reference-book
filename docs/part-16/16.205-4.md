# 16.205-4 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall , when contracting  by negotiation, insert the clause at [52.216-5](/part-52/52.216-5) , Price

Redetermination-Prospective, in solicitations  and contracts when a fixed-price contract is

contemplated and the conditions specified in [16.205-2](/part-16/16.205-2)  and [16.205-3](/part-16/16.205-3) (a) through (d) apply.
