# 16.501-1 Definitions

As used in this subpart-

Delivery-order contract  means a contract for supplies  that does not procure or specify a firm

quantity of supplies  (other than a minimum or maximum quantity) and that provides for the issuance

of orders for the delivery of supplies  during the period of the contract.

Task-order contract  means a contract for services that does not procure or specify a firm quantity of

services (other than a minimum or maximum quantity) and that provides for the issuance of orders

for the performance of tasks during the period of the contract.
