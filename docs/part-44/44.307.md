# 44.307 Reports

The ACO shall  distribute copies of CPSR reports; notifications granting, withholding, or withdrawing

system approval; and Government recommendations for improvement of an approved system,

including the contractor’s response, to at least-

(a) The cognizant contract audit office;

(b) Activities prescribed by the cognizant agency; and

(c) The contractor (except that furnishing copies of the contractor’s response is optional).
