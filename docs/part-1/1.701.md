# 1.701 Definition

Determination and Findings  means a special form of written approval by an authorized official that

is required by statute or regulation as a prerequisite to taking certain contract actions. The

"determination" is a conclusion or decision supported by the "findings.’’ The findings are statements

of fact or rationale essential to support the determination and must  cover each requirement of the

statute or regulation.
