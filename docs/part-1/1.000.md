# 1.000 Scope of part

This part sets forth basic policies and general information about the Federal Acquisition  Regulations

System including purpose, authority, applicability, issuance, arrangement, numbering,

dissemination, implementation, supplementation, maintenance, administration, and deviation.

subparts  [1.2](/part-1/1.2) ,[1.3](/part-1/1.3), and [1.4](/part-1/1.4) prescribe administrative procedures for maintaining the FAR System.
