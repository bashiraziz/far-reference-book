# 47.303-2 F.o.b. origin, contractor’s facility

(a) Explanation of delivery term. "F.o.b. origin, contractor’s facility" means free of expense to the

Government delivered on board the indicated type of conveyance of the carrier  (or of the

Government if specified) at the designated facility, on the named street or highway, in the city,

county, and State from which the shipment  will be made.
