# 47.206 Preparation of solicitations and contracts

(a) Contracting officers  shall  prepare solicitations  and contracts for transportation or for

transportation-related services as prescribed elsewhere in the FAR for fixed-price service contracts

to the extent that those requirements are applicable and not inconsistent with the requirements in

subpart  [47.2](/part-47/47.2) .
