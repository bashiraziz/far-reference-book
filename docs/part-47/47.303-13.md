# 47.303-13 (b), except that, in addition, the contractor shall  obtain and dispatch to the Government an

insurance  policy or certificate providing the amount and extent of marine insurance  coverage

specified in the contract or agreed upon by the Government contracting officer .

(c) Contract clause . The contracting officer  shall  insert in solicitations  and contracts the clause at

[52.247-42](/part-52/52.247-42) , C.i.f. (Cost, insurance , freight ) Destination, when the delivery term is c.i.f. destination.
