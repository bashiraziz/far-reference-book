# 47.305-17 Returnable cylinders

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at 52.247-66 , Returnable Cylinders, in a solicitation

and contract whenever the contract involves the purchase of gas in contractor-furnished returnable

cylinders and the contractor retains title to the cylinders.
