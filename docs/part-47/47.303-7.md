# 47.303-7 F.o.b. destination, within consignee’s premises

(a) Explanation of delivery term. "F.o.b. destination, within consignee’s premises" means free of

expense to the Government delivered and laid down within the doors of the consignee’s premises,

including delivery to specific rooms within a building if so specified.
