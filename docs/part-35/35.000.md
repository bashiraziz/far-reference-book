# 35.000 Scope of part

(a) This part prescribes policies and procedures of special application to research and development

(R&D) contracting .

(b) R&D integral to acquisition  of major systems  is covered in part  34 . Independent research and

development  (IR&D) is covered at [31.205-18](/part-31/31.205-18) .
