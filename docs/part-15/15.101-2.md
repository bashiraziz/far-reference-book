# 15.101-2 Lowest price technically acceptable source selection process

(a) The lowest price technically acceptable source selection process is appropriate when best value

is expected to result from selection of the technically acceptable proposal with the lowest evaluated

price.

(b) When using the lowest price technically acceptable process, the following apply:

(1) The evaluation factors and significant subfactors that establish the requirements of acceptability

shall  be set forth in the solicitation . Solicitations  shall  specify that award will be made on the basis

of the lowest evaluated price of proposals meeting or exceeding the acceptability standards for non-

cost factors. If the contracting officer  documents the file pursuant to 15.304 (c)(3)(iii), past
