# 15.509 Forms

Optional Form (OF) 307, Contract Award, Standard Form (SF) 26 , Award/Contract, or SF 33 ,

Solicitation , Offer  and Award, may be used to award negotiated contracts in which the signature  of

both parties on a single document is appropriate. Note however, if using the SF 26 for a negotiated

procurement , block 18 is not to be used. If these forms are not used, the award document shall

incorporate the agreement and award language from the OF 307.
