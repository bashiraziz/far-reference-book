# 15.204-4 Part III-List of Documents, Exhibits, and Other Attachments

Section J, List of attachments . The contracting officer  shall  list the title, date, and number of pages

for each attached document, exhibit, and other attachment. Cross-references to material in other

sections may be inserted, as appropriate.
