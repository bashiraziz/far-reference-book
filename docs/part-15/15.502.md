# 15.502 Applicability

This subpart applies to competitive proposals, as described in [6.102](/part-6/6.102) (b), and a combination of

competitive procedures, as described in [6.102](/part-6/6.102) (c). The procedures in [15.504](/part-15/15.504) ,[15.506](/part-15/15.506) ,[15.507](/part-15/15.507) ,[15.508](/part-15/15.508) ,

and [15.509](/part-15/15.509) , with reasonable modification, should  be followed for sole source acquisitions  and

acquisitions  described in [6.102](/part-6/6.102) (d)(1) and (2).
