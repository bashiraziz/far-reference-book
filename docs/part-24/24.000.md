# 24.000 Scope of part

This part prescribes policies and procedures that apply requirements of the Privacy Act of1974 ( 5

U.S.C. 552a ) (the Act) and OMB CircularNo.A-130, December 12,1985, to Government contracts and

cites the Freedom of Information Act ( 5 U.S.C.552 , as amended).

Subpart [24.1](/part-24/24.1)  - Protection of Individual Privacy
