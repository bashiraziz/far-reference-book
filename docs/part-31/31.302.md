# 31.302 General

The OMB Uniform Guidance at 2 CFR part 200 , subpart E and appendix III, provides principles for

determining the costs applicable to research and development, training, and other work performed

by educational institutions (defined as institutions of higher education in the OMB Uniform Guidance

at 2 CFR part 200 , subpart A, and 20 U.S.C. 1001 ) under contracts with the Government.
