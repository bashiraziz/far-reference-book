# 31.002 Availability of accounting guide

Contractors needing assistance in developing or improving their accounting systems and procedures

may request a copy of the Defense Contract Audit Agency Pamphlet No.7641.90, Information for

Contractors. The pamphlet is available via the Internet at http://www.dcaa.mil .
