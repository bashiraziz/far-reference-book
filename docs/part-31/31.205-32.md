# 31.205-32 Precontract costs

Precontract costs  means costs  incurred before the effective date of the contract directly pursuant to

the negotiation and in anticipation of the contract award when such incurrence is necessary to

comply with the proposed contract delivery schedule. These costs  are allowable to the extent that

they would have been allowable if incurred after the date of the contract (see 31.109 ).
