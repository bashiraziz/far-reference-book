# 31.702 General

The OMB Uniform Guidance at 2 CFR part 200 , subpart E and appendix IV, sets forth principles for

determining the costs applicable to work performed by nonprofit organizations (as defined in the

OMB Uniform Guidance at 2 CFR part 200 ) under contracts (as well as grants and other

agreements) with the Government. See 31.108  for exceptions to the cost principles for nonprofit

organizations.
