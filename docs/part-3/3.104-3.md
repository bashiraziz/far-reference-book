# 3.104-3 , knowing that the official  has not complied with 3.104-3 (c)(1), is subject to the criminal,

civil, or administrative penalties set forth in 41 U.S.C. 2105 .

(c) An official  who refuses to terminate employment discussions (see 3.104-5 ) may be subject to

agency administrative actions under 5 CFR 2635.604(d)  if the official ’s disqualification from

participation in a particular procurement  interferes substantially with the individual’s ability to

perform assigned duties.
