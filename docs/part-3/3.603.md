# 3.603 Responsibilities of the contracting officer

(a) Before awarding a contract, the contracting officer  shall  obtain an authorization under [3.602](/part-3/3.602)  if-

(1) The contracting officer  knows, or has reason to believe, that a prospective contractor is one to

which award is otherwise prohibited under [3.601](/part-3/3.601) ; and

(2) There is a most compelling reason to make an award to that prospective contractor.

(b) The contracting officer  shall  comply with the requirements and guidance in subpart  [9.5](/part-9/9.5)  before

awarding a contract to an organization owned or substantially owned or controlled by Government

employees.
