# 3.906 Contract clause.


            The contracting officer shall insert the clause at [52.203-17](./52.203-17.md), Contractor Employee Whistleblower Rights, in all solicitations and contracts, except solicitations and contracts of DoD, NASA, the Coast Guard, or applicable elements of the intelligence community (see [3.900](./3.900.md)(a)).
