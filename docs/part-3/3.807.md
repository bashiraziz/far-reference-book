# 3.807 Civil penalties

Agencies  shall  impose and collect civil penalties pursuant to the provisions of the Program Fraud

and Civil Remedies Act, 31 U.S.C.3803  (except subsection(c)), 3804-3808, and 3812, insofar as the

provisions therein are not inconsistent with the requirements of this subpart.
