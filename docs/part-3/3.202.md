# 3.202 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.203-3](/part-52/52.203-3) , Gratuities, in solicitations  and contracts

with a value exceeding the simplified acquisition threshold , except those for personal services and

those between military departments or defense agencies and foreign governments that do not

obligate any funds appropriated to the Department of Defense.
