# 3.404 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.203-5](/part-52/52.203-5) , Covenant Against Contingent Fees , in all

solicitations  and contracts exceeding the simplified acquisition threshold , other than those for

commercial products  or commercial services  (see parts  2  and 12).
