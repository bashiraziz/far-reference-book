# 3.804 Policy.

The contracting officer shall obtain certifications and disclosures as required by the provision at [52.203-11](./52.203-11.md), Certification and Disclosure Regarding Payments to Influence Certain Federal Transactions, prior to the award of any contract exceeding $200,000.
