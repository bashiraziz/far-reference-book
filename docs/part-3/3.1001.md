# 3.1001 Definitions.

As used in this subpart-


            *Subcontract* means
any contract entered into by a subcontractor to furnish supplies
or services for performance of a prime contract or a subcontract.


            *Subcontractor* means
any supplier, distributor, vendor, or firm that furnished supplies
or services to or for a prime contractor or another subcontractor.


            *United States* means the 50 States, the
District of Columbia, and outlying areas.
