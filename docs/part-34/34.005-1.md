# 34.005-1 Competition

(a) The program manager shall , throughout the acquisition  process, promote full and open

competition  and sustain effective competition  between alternative major system  concepts and

sources, as long as it is economically beneficial and practicable to do so. Notice of the proposed

acquisition  shall  be given the broadest and most effective circulation practicable throughout the

business, academic, and Government communities. Foreign contractors, technology, and equipment

may be considered when it is feasible and permissible to do so.

(b) The contracting officer  should  time solicitation  issuance and contract award to maintain

continuity of concept development during the transition from with-drawing concept proposer to new

contractor.
