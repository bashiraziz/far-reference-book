# 32.304-6 Other collateral security

The following are examples of other forms of security that, although seldom invoked under

guaranteed loans , may be required when considered necessary for protection of the Government

interest:

(a) Mortgages on fixed assets.

(b) Liens against inventories.

(c) Endorsements.

(d) Guarantees.

(e) Subordinations or standbys of other indebtedness.
