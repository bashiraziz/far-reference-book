# 32.608-1 Interest charges

Unless specified otherwise in the clause at 52.232-17 , Interest, interest charges shall  apply to any

contract debt unpaid after 30 days from the issuance of a demand unless-

(a) The contract is a kind excluded under 32.611 ; or

(b) The contract or debt has been exempted from interest charges under agency procedures.
