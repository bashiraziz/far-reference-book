# 32.706-2 Clauses for limitation of cost or funds

(a) The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.232-20](/part-52/52.232-20) , Limitation of Cost, in solicitations

and contracts if a fully funded cost-reimbursement contract is contemplated, whether or not the

contract provides for payment of a fee.

(b) The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.232-22](/part-52/52.232-22) , Limitation of Funds, in solicitations

and contracts if an incrementally funded cost-reimbursement contract is contemplated.
