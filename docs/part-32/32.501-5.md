# 32.501-5 Other protective terms

If the contracting officer  considers it necessary for protection of the Governmentâ€™s interest,

protective terms such as the following may be used in addition to the Progress Payments clause of

the contract:

(a) Personal or corporate guarantees.

(b) Subordinations or standbys of indebtedness.

(c) Special bank accounts.

(d) Protective covenants of the kinds in paragraph (p) of the clause at [52.232-12](/part-52/52.232-12) , Advance Payments.

(e) A provision, included in the solicitation  and resultant contract when first article testing  is

required (see subpart  [9.3](/part-9/9.3) ), limiting progress payments on first article  work by a stated amount or

percentage.
