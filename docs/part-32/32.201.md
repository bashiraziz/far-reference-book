# 32.201 Statutory authority

10 U.S.C. 3805  and 41 U.S.C.4505  provide that payment for commercial products  or commercial

services  may be made under such terms and conditions as the head of the agency  determines are

appropriate or customary in the commercial marketplace and are in the best interest of the United

States .
