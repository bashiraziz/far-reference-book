# 32.703-2 Contracts conditioned upon availability of funds

(a) Fiscal year contracts.  The contracting officer  may initiate a contract action  properly chargeable

to funds of the new fiscal year before these funds are available, provided that the contract includes

the clause at 52.232-18 , Availability of Funds (see 32.706-1 (a)). This authority may be used only for

operation and maintenance and continuing services ( e.g., rentals, utilities, and supply items not

financed by stock funds)-

(1) Necessary for normal operations; and

(2) For which Congress previously had consistently appropriated funds, unless specific statutory

authority exists permitting applicability to other requirements.

(b) Indefinite-quantity or requirements contracts.  A one-year indefinite-quantity or requirements

contract for services that is funded by annual appropriations may extend beyond the fiscal year in

which it begins; provided, that-

(1) Any specified minimum quantities are certain to be ordered in the initial fiscal year (see 37.106 )
