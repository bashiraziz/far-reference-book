# 32.503-11 Adjustments for price reduction

(a) If a retroactive downward price reduction occurs under a redeterminable contract that provides

for progress payments, the contracting officer  shall -

(1) Determine the refund due and obtain repayment from the contractor for the excess of payments

made for delivered items over amounts due as recomputed at the reduced prices; and

(2) Increase the unliquidated progress payments amount for overdeductions made from the

contractorâ€™s billings for items delivered.

(b) The contracting officer  shall  also increase the unliquidated progress payments amount if the

contractor makes an interim or voluntary price reduction under a redeterminable or incentive

contract.
