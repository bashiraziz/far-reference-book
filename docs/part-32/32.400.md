# 32.400 Scope of subpart

This subpart provides policies and procedures for advance payments on prime contracts and

subcontracts. It does not include policies and procedures for advance payments for the types of

transactions listed in [32.404](/part-32/32.404) . This subpart does not apply to commercial advance payments, which

are subject to subpart  [32.2](/part-32/32.2) .
