# 32.903 Responsibilities

(a) Agency heads -

(1) Must  establish the policies and procedures necessary to implement this subpart;

(2) May prescribe additional standards for establishing invoice payment  due dates  (see 32.904 )

necessary to support agency programs and foster prompt payment to contractors;

(3) May adopt different payment procedures in order to accommodate unique circumstances,

provided that such procedures are consistent with the policies in this subpart;

(4) Must  inform contractors of points of contact within their cognizant payment offices to enable

contractors to obtain status of invoices ; and
