# 32.502-1 Use of customary progress payments

The contracting officer  may use a Progress Payments clause in solicitations  and contracts, in

accordance with this subpart. The contracting officer  must  reject as nonresponsive bids conditioned

on progress payments when the solicitation  did not provide for progress payments.
