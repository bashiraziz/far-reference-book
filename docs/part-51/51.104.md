# 51.104 Furnishing assistance to contractors

After receiving an activity address code , the contracting officer  will notify the appropriate GSA

regional office or military activity, which will contact the contractor and-

(a) Provide initial copies of ordering information and instructions; and

(b) When necessary, assist the contractor in preparing and submitting, as appropriate-

(1) The initial FEDSTRIP or MILSTRIP requisitions, the Optional Form 347 , or the agency-approved

forms;

(2) A completed GSA Form 457, FSS Publications Mailing List Application, so that the contractor will

automatically receive current copies of required publications; or

(3) A completed GSA Form 3525, Application for Customer Supply Center Services and (Address

Change).
