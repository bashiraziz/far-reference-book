# 51.107 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.251-1](/part-52/52.251-1) , Government Supply Sources, in

solicitations  and contracts when the contracting officer  authorizes the contractor to acquire supplies

or services from a Government supply source.
