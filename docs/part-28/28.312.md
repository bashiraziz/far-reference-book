# 28.312 Contract clause for insurance of leased motor vehicles

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.228-8](/part-52/52.228-8) , Liability and Insurance -Leased Motor

Vehicles, in solicitations  and contracts for the leasing of motor vehicles (see subpart  [8.11](/part-8/8.11) ).
