# 28.203-4 Solicitation provision and contract clause

(a)Insert the provision at [52.228-17](/part-52/52.228-17) , Individual Surety —Pledge of Assets ( Bid Guarantee ), in

solicitations  that require the submission of a bid guarantee .

(b)Insert the clause at [52.228-11](/part-52/52.228-11) , Individual Surety —Pledge of Assets, in solicitations  and contracts

that require the submission of performance or payment bonds .
