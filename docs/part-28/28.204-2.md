# 28.204-2 Certified or cashier’s checks, bank drafts, money orders, or currency

Any person required to furnish a bond  has an option  to furnish a certified or cashier’s check, bank

draft, Post Office money order, or currency, in an amount equal to the penal sum  of the bond ,

instead of furnishing surety  or sureties  on the bonds . Those furnishing checks, drafts, or money

orders shall  draw them to the order of the appropriate Federal agency .
