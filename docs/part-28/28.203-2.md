# 28.203-2 Substitution of assets

An individual surety  may request the Government to accept a substitute asset for that currently

pledged by submitting a written request, including a revised SF 28 , to the responsible contracting

officer . Following the requirements set forth in 28.203-1 , the contracting officer  may agree to the

substitution of assets upon determining that the substitute assets to be pledged are adequate to

protect the outstanding bond  or guarantee obligations.
