# 28.311-1 Contract clause

In accordance with agency acquisition  regulations, the contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at

52.228-7 , Insurance -Liability to Third Persons, in solicitations  and contracts, other than those for

construction  contracts and those for architect-engineer services , when a cost-reimbursement

contract is contemplated.
