# 28.106-4 Contract clause

(a) The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.228-2](/part-52/52.228-2) , Additional Bond  Security, in

solicitations  and contracts when bonds  are required.

(b) In accordance with secti on 806(a)(3) of Public Law 102-190, as amended by sections 2091 and

8105 of Public Law 103-355 ( 10 U.S.C. 4601 note  prec.), the contracting officer  shall  insert the

clause at [52.228-12](/part-52/52.228-12) , Prospective Subcontractor Requests for Bonds , in solicitations  and contracts

with respect to which a payment bond  will be furnished pursuant to 40 U.S.C chapter 31, subchapter

III, Bonds  (see [28.102-1](/part-28/28.102-1) ), except for contracts for the acquisition  of commercial products  or

commercial services  as defined in subpart  [2.1](/part-2/2.1) .
