# 40.202-1 Scope

(a) Section [40.202](/part-40/40.202)  prescribes policies and procedures regarding the procurement  and operation of

unmanned aircraft systems , which includes unmanned aircraft  (i.e., drones) and associated

elements.

(b) The authorities in [40.203](/part-40/40.203)  expire on December 22, 2028 (section 1833 of Pub. L. 118-31, 41

U.S.C. 3901  note prec.).
