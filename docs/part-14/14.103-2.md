# 14.103-2 Limitations

No awards shall  be made as a result of sealed bidding unless-

(a) Bids have been solicited as required by subpart  [14.2](/part-14/14.2) ;

(b) Bids have been submitted as required by subpart  [14.3](/part-14/14.3) ;

(c) The requirements of [1.602-1](/part-1/1.602-1) (b) and part  6  have been met; and

(d) An award is made to the responsible bidder (see [9.1](/part-9/9.1)) whose bid is responsive to the terms of the

invitation for bids and is most advantageous to the Government, considering only price and the price

related factors included in the invitation, as provided in subpart  [14.4](/part-14/14.4) .
