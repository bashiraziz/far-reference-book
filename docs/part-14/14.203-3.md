# 14.203-3 Master solicitation

The master solicitation  is provided to potential sources who are requested to retain it for continued

and repetitive use. Individual solicitations  must  reference the date of the current master solicitation

and identify any changes. The contracting officer  must -

(a) Make available copies of the master solicitation  on request; and

(b) Provide the cognizant contract administration activity a current copy of the master solicitation .
