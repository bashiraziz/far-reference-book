# 14.402-2 Classified bids

The general public may not attend bid openings for classified acquisitions . A bidder or its

representative may attend and record the results if the individual has the appropriate security

clearance. The contracting officer  also may make the bids available at a later time to properly

cleared individuals who represent bidders. No public record shall  be made of bids or bid prices

received in response to classified invitations for bids.
