# 14.408-3 Prompt payment discounts

(a) Prompt payment discounts shall  not be considered in the evaluation of bids. However, any

discount offered will form a part of the award, and will be taken by the payment center if payment is

made within the discount period specified by the bidder. As an alternative to indicating a discount in

conjunction with the offer , bidders may prefer to offer  discounts on individual invoices .

(b) See 32.111 (b)(1), which prescribes the contract clause  at 52.232-8 , Discounts for Prompt

Payment.
