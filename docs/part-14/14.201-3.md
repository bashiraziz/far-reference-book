# 14.201-3 PartII-Contract clauses

Section I, Contract clauses . The contracting officer  shall  include in this section the clauses required

by law or by this regulation and any additional clauses expected to apply to any resulting contract, if

these clauses are not required to be included in any other section of the uniform contract format.
