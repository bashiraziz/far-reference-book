# 14.103-1 General

(a) Sealed bidding shall  be used whenever the conditions in 6.401 (a) are met. This requirement

applies to any proposed contract action under part  6 .

(b) Sealed bidding may be used for classified acquisitions  if its use does not violate agency security

requirements.

(c) The policy for pricing  modifications of sealed bid contract appears in 15.403-4 (a)(1)(iii).
