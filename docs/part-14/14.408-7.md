# 14.408-7 Documentation of award

(a) The contracting officer  shall  document compliance with 14.103-2  in the contract file.

(b) The documentation shall  either state that the accepted bid was the lowest bid received, or list all

lower bids with reasons for their rejection in sufficient detail to justify the award.

(c) When an award is made after receipt of equal low bids, the documentation shall  describe how the

tie was broken.
