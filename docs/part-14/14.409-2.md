# 14.409-2 Award of classified contracts

In addition to 14.409-1 , if classified information  was furnished or created in connection with the

solicitation , the contracting officer  shall  advise the unsuccessful bidders, including any who did not

bid, to take disposition action in accordance with agency procedures. The name of the successful

bidder and the contract price will be furnished to unsuccessful bidders only upon request.

Information regarding a classified award shall  not be furnished by telephone.
