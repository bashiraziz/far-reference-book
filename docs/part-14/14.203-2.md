# 14.203-2 Dissemination of information concerning invitations for bids

Procedures concerning display of invitations for bids in a public place, information releases to

newspapers and trade journals, paid advertisements, and synopsizing through the Governmentwide

point of entry (GPE ) are set forth in [5.101](/part-5/5.101)  and subpart  [5.2](/part-5/5.2) .
