# 14.202-6 Final review of invitations for bids

Each invitation for bids shall  be thoroughly reviewed before issuance to detect and correct

discrepancies or ambiguities that could limit competition or result in the receipt of nonresponsive

bids. Contracting officers  are responsible for the reviews.
