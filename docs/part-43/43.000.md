# 43.000 Scope of part

This part prescribes policies and procedures for preparing and processing contract modifications  for

all types of contracts including construction  and architect-engineer contracts. It does not apply to-

(a) Orders for supplies  or services not otherwise changing the terms of contracts or agreements

(e.g., delivery orders  under indefinite-delivery contracts); or

(b) Modifications for extraordinary contractual relief (see subpart  [50.1](/part-50/50.1) ).
