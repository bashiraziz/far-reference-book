# 4.606 (a)(1)

(11) Letters of obligation used in the A-76 process.

(d) Agencies not subject to the FAR. Agencies not subject to the FAR may be required by other

authority (e.g., statute, OMB, or internal agency policy) to report certain information to FPDS. Those

agencies not subject to the FAR must  first receive approval from the FPDS Program Office prior to

reporting to FPDS.
