# 37.115-3 Solicitation provision

The contracting officer  shall  insert the provision at [52.237-10](/part-52/52.237-10) , Identification of Uncompensated

Overtime , in all solicitations  valued above the simplified acquisition threshold , for professional or

technical services to be acquired on the basis of the number of hours to be provided.
