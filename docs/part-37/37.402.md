# 37.402 Contracting officer responsibilities

Contracting officers  shall  obtain evidence of insurability concerning medical liability insurance  from

the apparent successful offeror  prior to contract award and shall  obtain evidence of insurance

demonstrating the required coverage prior to commencement of performance.
