# 42.709-1 Scope

(a) This section implements 10 U.S.C. 3743  and 41 U.S.C. 4303 . It covers the assessment of

penalties against contractors which include unallowable indirect costs  in-

(1) Final indirect cost rate  proposals; or

(2) The final statement of costs incurred or estimated to be incurred under a fixed-price incentive

contract.

(b) This section applies to all contracts in excess of $1 million , except fixed-price contracts without

cost incentives or any firm-fixed-price contracts for the purchase of commercial products  or

commercial services .
