# 42.301 General

When a contract is assigned for administration under subpart  [42.2](/part-42/42.2) , the contract administration

office  (CAO) shall  perform contract administration functions in accordance with 48 CFR Chapter 1,

the contract terms, and, unless otherwise agreed to in an interagency agreement (see [42.002](/part-42/42.002) ), the

applicable regulations of the servicing agency .
