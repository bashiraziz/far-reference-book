# 42.203 Contract administration services directory.

The Defense Contract Management Agency (DCMA) maintains the Federal Directory of Contract Administration Services Components. The directory lists the names and telephone numbers of those DCMA and other agency offices that offer contract administration services within designated geographic areas and at specified contractor plants. Federal agencies may access it on the Internet at https://piee.eb.mil/pcm/xhtml/unauth/index.xhtml. For additional information contact- Defense Contract Management Agency 3901 A Avenue Building 10500 Ft. Lee, VA 23801-1809.
