# 19.502-7 Inclusion of Federal Prison Industries, Inc

When using competitive procedures in accordance with [8.602](/part-8/8.602) (a)(4), agencies shall  include Federal

Prison Industries , Inc. (FPI), in the solicitation  process and consider a timely offer  from FPI.
