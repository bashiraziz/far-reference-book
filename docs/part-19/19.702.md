# 19.702 (a)(1)(iii)) or a size re-representation (see 19.301-2 (e)), the subcontracting goals apply from

the date of incorporation of the subcontracting plan into the contract and the contractor's

achievements must  be reported on the ISR (or the SF-294, if applicable) on a cumulative basis from

the date of incorporation of the subcontracting plan into the contract.
