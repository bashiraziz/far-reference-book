# 19.301-3 Rerepresentation by a contractor that represented itself as other than a small

business concern.

A contractor that represented itself as other than small before contract award may, but is not

required to, rerepresent its size status when-

(a) The conditions in [19.301-2](/part-19/19.301-2) (b) apply; and

(b) The contractor qualifies as a small business concern  under the applicable size standard in effect

at the time of its rerepresentation.
