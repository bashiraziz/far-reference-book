# 52.247-28 Contractor’s Invoices

As prescribed in 47.207-9 (c), insert the following clause in solicitations  and contracts for drayage or

other term con- tracts for transportation or for transportation-related services:

Contractor’s Invoices  (Apr 1984)

The Contractor shall submit itemized invoices  as instructed by the agency ordering services under

this contract. The Contractor shall annotate each invoice with the contract number and other

ordering office document identification.

(End of clause)
