# 52.252-4 Alterations in Contract

As prescribed in [52.107](/part-52/52.107) (d), insert the following clause in solicitations  and contracts in order to

revise or supplement, as necessary, other parts of the contract, or parts of the solicitation that apply

after contract award, except for any clause authorized for use with a deviation. Include clear

identification of what is being altered.

Alterations in Contract (Apr 1984)

Portions of this contract are altered as follows:

_____________________________________________ _____________________________________________

_____________________________________________

(End of clause)
