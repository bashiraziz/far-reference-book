# 52.236-8 Other Contracts

As prescribed in [36.508](/part-36/36.508)  , insert the following clause:

Other Contracts (Apr 1984)

The Government may undertake or award other contracts for additional work at or near the site of

the work under this contract. The Contractor shall fully cooperate with the other

contractors and with Government employees and shall carefully adapt scheduling and performing

the work under this contract to accommodate the additional work, heeding any direction that may be

provided by the Contracting Officer. The Contractor shall not commit or permit any act that will

interfere with the performance of work by any other contractor or by Government employees.

(End of clause)
