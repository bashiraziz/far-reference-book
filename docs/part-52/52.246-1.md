# 52.246-1 Contractor Inspection Requirements

As prescribed in [46.301](/part-46/46.301)  , insert the following clause:

Contractor Inspection Requirements (Apr 1984)

The Contractor is responsible for performing or having performed all inspections  and tests

necessary to substantiate that the supplies or services furnished under this contract conform to

contract requirements, including any applicable technical requirements for specified manufacturers’

parts. This clause takes precedence over any Government inspection and testing required in the

contract’s specifications, except for specialized inspections  or tests specified to be performed solely

by the Government.

(End of clause)
