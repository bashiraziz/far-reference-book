# 52.247-9 Agreed Weight-General Freight

As prescribed in 47.207-4 (a)(1) , insert the following clause in solicitations  and contracts for

transportation or for transportation-related services when the shipping activity determines the

weight of shipments  of freight other than household goods or office furniture:

Agreed Weight-General Freight (Apr 1984)

The shipping activity shall determine the weight of each shipment. The weight shall be shown on the

covering shipping document and shall be accepted by the Contractor as the agreed weight.

(End of clause)
