# 52.247-56 Transit Arrangements

As prescribed in 47.305-13  (a)(3)(ii), insert the following provision in solicitations  when benefits may

accrue to the Government because transit arrangements may apply:

Transit Arrangements (Apr 1984)

The lowest appropriate common carrier transportation costs, including offeror’s through transit

rates and charges when applicable, from offeror’s shipping points, via the transit point, to the

ultimate destination will be used in evaluating offers .
