# 52.214-34 Submission of Offers in the English Language

As prescribed in [14.201-6](/part-14/14.201-6) (w), insert the following provision:

Submission of Offers  in the English Language (Apr 1991)

Offers  submitted in response to this solicitation shall be in the English language. Offers  received in

other than English shall be rejected.

(End of provision)
