# 52.247-21 Contractor Liability for Personal Injury and/or Property Damage

As prescribed in [47.207-7](/part-47/47.207-7) (c), insert the following clause in solicitations  and contracts for

transportation or for transportation-related services:

Contractor Liability for Personal Injury and/or Property Damage (Apr 1984)

(a) The Contractor assumes responsibility for all damage or injury to persons or property

occasioned through the use, maintenance, and operation of the Contractor’s vehicles or other

equipment by, or the action of, the Contractor or the Contractor’s employees and agents.

(b) The Contractor, at the Contractor’s expense, shall maintain adequate public liability and

property damage insurance during the continuance of this contract, insuring the Contractor against

all claims  for injury or damage.

(c) The Contractor shall maintain Workers’ Compensation and other legally required insurance

with respect to the Contractor’s own employees and agents.

(d) The Government shall in no event be liable or responsible for damage or injury to any person

or property occasioned through the use, maintenance, or operation of any vehicle or other

equipment by, or the action of, the Contractor or the Contractor’s employees and agents in

performing under this contract, and the Government shall be indemnified and saved harmless

against claims  for damage or injury in such cases.

(End of clause)
