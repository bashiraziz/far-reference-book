# 52.241-1 Electric Service Territory Compliance Representation

As prescribed in [41.501](/part-41/41.501) (b), insert a provision substantially the same as the following:

Electric Service Territory Compliance Representation (May 1999)

(a) Section 8093 of Public Law100-202 generally requires purchases of electricity by any

department, agency, or instrumentality of the United States to be consistent with State law

governing the provision of electric utility service, including State utility commission rulings and

electric utility franchises or service territories established pursuant to State statute, State

regulation, or State-approved territorial agreements.

(b) By signing this offer, the offeror represents that this offer to sell electricity is consistent with

Section 8093 of Public Law100-202.

(c) Upon request of the Contracting Officer, the offeror shall submit supporting legal and factual

rationale for this representation.

(End of clause)
