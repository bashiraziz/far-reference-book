# 52.217-7 Option for Increased Quantity-Separately Priced Line Item

As prescribed in [17.208](/part-17/17.208) (e), insert a clause substantially the same as the following:

Option for Increased Quantity-Separately Priced Line Item (Mar 1989)

The Government may require the delivery of the numbered line item, identified in the Schedule as an

option item, in the quantity and at the price stated in the Schedule. The Contracting Officer may

exercise the option by written notice to the Contractor within [insert in the clause the period of time

in which the Contracting Officer has to exercise the option ]. Delivery of added items shall continue

at the same rate that like items are called for under the contract, unless the parties otherwise agree.

(End of clause)
