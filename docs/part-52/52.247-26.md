# 52.247-26 Government Direction and Marking

As prescribed in 47.207-8 (a)(3) , insert the following clause in solicitations  and contracts for

transportation or for transportation-related services when office relocations are involved:

Government Direction and Marking (Apr 1984)

The agency being relocated shall tag or mark property, showing floor, room number, and location

where property is to be placed in the new building. The agency shall provide sufficient personnel to

direct the Contractorâ€™s personnel in the placement of the property at destination.

(End of clause)
