# 52.236-18 Work Oversight in Cost-Reimbursement Construction Contracts

As prescribed in 36.518  , insert the following clause in solicitations  and contracts when cost-

reimbursement construction contracts are contemplated:

Work Oversight in Cost-Reimbursement Construction Contracts (Apr 1984)

The extent and character of the work to be done by the Contractor shall be subject to the general

supervision, direction, control, and approval of the Contracting Officer.

(End of clause)
