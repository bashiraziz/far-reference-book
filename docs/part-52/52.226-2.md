# 52.226-2 Historically Black College or University and Minority Institution Representation

As prescribed in [26.304](/part-26/26.304)  , insert the following provision:

Historically Black College or University and Minority Institution Representation (Oct 2014)

(a) Definitions . As used in this provision—

Historically black college or university  means an institution determined by the Secretary of

Education to meet the requirements of 34 CFR 608.2 .

Minority institution  means an institution of higher education meeting the requirements of

Section 365(3) of the Higher Education Act of1965 ( 20 U.S.C.1067k ), including a Hispanic-serving

institution of higher education, as defined in Section502(a) of the Act ( 20 U.S.C.1101a ).

(b) Representation . The offeror represents that it—

□ is □ is not a historically black college or university;

□ is □ is not a minority institution.

(End of provision)
