# 52.247-50 No Evaluation of Transportation Costs

As prescribed in [47.305-5](/part-47/47.305-5) (c)(1) , insert the following provision in solicitations  when exact

destinations are not known and it is impractical to establish tentative or general delivery places for

the purpose of evaluating transportation costs:

No Evaluation of Transportation Costs (Apr 1984)

Costs of transporting supplies to be delivered under this contract will not be an evaluation factor for

award.

(End of clause)
