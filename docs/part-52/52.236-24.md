# 52.236-24 Work Oversight in Architect-Engineer Contracts

As prescribed in 36.609-3  , insert the following clause:

Work Oversight in Architect-Engineer Contracts (Apr 1984)

The extent and character of the work to be done by the Contractor shall be subject to the general

oversight, supervision, direction, control, and approval of the Contracting Officer.

(End of clause)
