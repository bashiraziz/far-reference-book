# 52.223-17 Affirmative Procurement of EPA-designated Items in Service and Construction

Contracts.

As prescribed in 23.406 (e), insert the following clause:

Affirmative Procurement of EPA-designated Items in Service and Construction Contracts (Aug 2018)

(a) In the performance of this contract, the Contractor shall make maximum use of products

containing recovered materials  that are EPA-designated items unless the product cannot be

acquired-

(1) Competitively within a timeframe providing for compliance with the contract performance

schedule;

(2) Meeting contract performance requirements; or

(3) At a reasonable price.

Guidelines web site,

EPA-designated items is available at

http://https://www.epa.gov/smm/comprehensive-procurement-guideline-cpg-program .

(End of clause)
