# 52.222-25 Affirmative Action Compliance

As prescribed in [22.810](/part-22/22.810) (d), insert the following provision:

Affirmative Action Compliance (Apr 1984)

The offeror represents that-

(a) It □ has developed and has on file, □ has not developed and does not have on file, at each

establishment, affirmative action programs required by the rules and regulations of the Secretary of

Labor ( 41 CFR 60-1 and 60-2 ); or

(b) It □ has not previously had contracts subject to the written affirmative action programs

requirement of the rules and regulations of the Secretary of Labor.

(End of provision)
