# 52.215-16 Facilities Capital Cost of Money

As prescribed in [15.408](/part-15/15.408) (h), insert the following provision:

Facilities Capital Cost of Money (June 2003)

(a) Facilities capital cost of money will be an allowable cost under the contemplated contract, if

the criteria for allowability in FAR [31.205-10](/part-31/31.205-10) (b) are met. One of the allowability criteria requires the

prospective Contractor to propose facilities capital cost of money in its offer.

(b) If the prospective Contractor does not propose this cost, the resulting contract will include

the clause Waiver of Facilities Capital Cost of Money.

(End of provision)
