# 52.215-17 Waiver of Facilities Capital Cost of Money

As prescribed in [15.408](/part-15/15.408) (i), insert the following clause:

Waiver of Facilities Capital Cost of Money (Oct 1997)

The Contractor did not include facilities capital cost of money as a proposed cost of this contract.

Therefore, it is an unallowable cost under this contract.

(End of clause)
