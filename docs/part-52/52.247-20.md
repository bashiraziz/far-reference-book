# 52.247-20 Estimated Quantities or Weights for Evaluation of Offers

As prescribed in [47.207-6](/part-47/47.207-6) (c)(6) , insert the following provision in solicitations  for transportation or

for transportation-related services when quantities or weights of shipments  between each origin and

destination are not known, stating estimated quantity or weight for each origin/destination pair:

Estimated Quantities or Weights for Evaluation of Offers  (Apr 1984)
