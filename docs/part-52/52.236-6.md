# 52.236-6 Superintendence by the Contractor

As prescribed in [36.506](/part-36/36.506)  , insert the following clause:

Superintendence by the Contractor (Apr 1984)

At all times during performance of this contract and until the work is completed and accepted, the

Contractor shall directly superintend the work or assign and have on the worksite a competent

superintendent who is satisfactory to the Contracting Officer and has authority to act for the

Contractor.

(End of clause)
