# 52.247-45 F.o.b. Origin and/or F.o.b. Destination Evaluation

As prescribed in 47.305-2 (b), insert the following provision in solicitations  when offers  are solicited

on the basis of both f.o.b. origin and f.o.b. destination:

F.o.b. Origin and/or F.o.b. Destination Evaluation (Apr 1984)

Offers  are invited on the basis of both f.o.b. origin and f.o.b. destination, and the Government will

award on the basis the Contracting Officer determines to be most advantageous to the Government.

An offer on the basis of f.o.b. origin only or f.o.b. destination only is acceptable, but will be evaluated

only on the basis submitted.

(End of clause)
