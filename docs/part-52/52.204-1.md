# 52.204-1 Approval of Contract

As prescribed in [4.103](/part-4/4.103)  , insert the following clause:

Approval of Contract (Dec 1989)

This contract is subject to the written approval of [identify title of designated agency official here ]

and shall not be binding until so approved.

(End of clause)
