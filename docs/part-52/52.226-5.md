# 52.226-5 Restrictions on Subcontracting Outside Disaster or Emergency Area

As prescribed in 26.206 (c), insert the following clause:

Restrictions on Subcontracting Outside Disaster or Emergency Area (Nov 2007)

(a) Definitions.  The definitions of the following terms used in this clause are found in the Small

Business Administration regulations at 13 CFR 125.6(e) : cost of the contract, cost of contract

performance incurred for personnel, cost of manufacturing, cost of materials, personnel, and

subcontracting.

(b) The Contractor agrees that in performance of the contract in the case of a contract for-

(1) Services (except construction) . At least 50 percent of the cost of contract performance

incurred for personnel shall be expended for employees of the Contractor or employees of other
