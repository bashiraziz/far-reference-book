# 52.214-35 Submission of Offers in U.S. Currency

As prescribed in 14.201-6 (x), insert the following provision:

Submission of Offers  in U.S. Currency (Apr 1991)

Offers  submitted in response to this solicitation shall be in terms of U.S. dollars. Offers  received in

other than U.S. dollars shall be rejected.

(End of provision)
