# 52.219-2 Equal Low Bids

As prescribed in 19.309 (b), insert the following provision:

Equal Low Bids (Oct 1995)

(c) Failure to identify the labor surplus areas  as specified in paragraph (b) of this provision will

with the obligations of an LSA concern.
