# 52.241-3 Scope and Duration of Contract

As prescribed in [41.501](/part-41/41.501) (c)(2) , insert a clause substantially the same as the following:

Scope and Duration of Contract (Feb 1995)

(a) For the period _______________________, [ insert period of service ] the Contractor agrees to

furnish and the Government agrees to purchase _____________________ [ insert type of service ] utility

service in accordance with the applicable tariff(s), rules, and regulations as approved by the

applicable governing regulatory body and as set forth in the contract.

(b) It is expressly understood that neither the Contractor nor the Government is under any

obligation to continue any service under the terms and conditions of this contract beyond the

expiration date.

(c) The Contractor shall provide the Government with one complete set of rates, terms, and

conditions of service which are in effect as of the date of this contract and any subsequently

approved rates.

(d) The Contractor shall be paid at the applicable rate(s) under the tariff and the Government

shall be liable for the minimum monthly charge, if any, specified in this contract commencing with

the period in which service is initially furnished and continuing for the term of this contract. Any

minimum monthly charge specified in this contract shall be equitably prorated for the periods in

which commencement and termination of this contract become effective.

(End of clause)
