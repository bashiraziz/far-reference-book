# 52.247-4 Inspection of Shipping and Receiving Facilities

As prescribed in 47.207-1 (c), insert the following provision in solicitations  for transportation or for

transportation-related services when it is desired for offerors  to inspect the shipping, receiving, or

other sites to ensure realistic bids:

Inspection of Shipping and Receiving Facilities (Apr 1984)

(a) Offerors  are urged to inspect the shipping and receiving facilities where services are to be

performed and to satisfy themselves regarding all general and local conditions that may affect the

cost of contract performance.
