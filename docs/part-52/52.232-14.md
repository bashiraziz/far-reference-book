# 52.232-14 Notice of Availability of Progress Payments Exclusively for Small Business

Concerns.

As prescribed in 32.502-3 (b)(2) , insert the following provision in invitations for bids if it is

anticipated that (a) both small business concerns and others may submit bids in response to the

same invitation and (b) only the small business bidders would need progress payments:

Notice of Availability of Progress Payments Exclusively for Small Business Concerns (Apr 1984)

The Progress Payments clause will be available only to small business concerns. Any bid conditioned

upon inclusion of a progress payment clause in the resulting contract will be rejected as

nonresponsive if the bidder is not a small business concern.

(End of clause)
