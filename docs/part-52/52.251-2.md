# 52.251-2 Interagency Fleet Management System Vehicles and Related Services

As prescribed in [51.205](/part-51/51.205)  , insert the following clause:

Interagency Fleet Management System Vehicles and Related Services (Jan 1991)

The Contracting Officer may issue the Contractor an authorization to obtain interagency fleet

management system (IFMS) vehicles and related services for use in the performance of this

contract. The use, service, and maintenance of interagency fleet management system vehicles and

the use of related services by the Contractor shall be in accordance with 41 CFR 101-39  and 41 CFR

101-[38.301](/part-38/38.301) -1.

(End of clause)
