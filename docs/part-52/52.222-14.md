# 52.222-14 Disputes Concerning Labor Standards

As prescribed in [22.407](/part-22/22.407) (a), insert the following clause:

Disputes Concerning Labor Standards (Feb 1988)

The United States Department of Labor has set forth in 29 CFR parts 5, 6, and 7 procedures for

resolving disputes concerning labor standards requirements. Such disputes shall be resolved in

accordance with those procedures and not the Disputes clause of this contract. Disputes within the

meaning of this clause include disputes between the Contractor (or any of its subcontractors) and

the contracting agency, the U.S. Department of Labor, or the employees or their representatives.

(End of clause)
