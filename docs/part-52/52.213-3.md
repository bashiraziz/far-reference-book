# 52.213-3 Notice to Supplier

As prescribed in 13.302-5 (c), insert the following clause:

Notice to Supplier (Apr 1984)

This is a firm order ONLY if your price does not exceed the maximum line item or total price in the

Schedule. Submit invoices  to the Contracting Officer. If you cannot perform in exact accordance

with this order, Withhold Performance, and notify the Contracting Officer immediately, giving your

quotation.

(End of clause)
