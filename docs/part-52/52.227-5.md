# 52.227-5 Waiver of Indemnity

As prescribed in [27.201-2](/part-27/27.201-2) (e), insert the following clause:

Waiver of Indemnity (Apr 1984)

Any provision or clause of this contract to the contrary notwithstanding, the Government hereby

authorizes and consents to the use and manufacture, solely in performing this contract, of any

invention covered by the United States patents identified below and waives indemnification by the

Contractor with respect to such patents:

__________________________________________________ [ Contracting Officer identify the patents by

number or by other means if more appropriate .]

(End of clause)
