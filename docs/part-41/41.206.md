# 41.206 Interagency agreements

Agencies shall  use interagency agreements ( e.g., consolidated purchase, joint use, or cross-service

agreements) when acquiring utility service or facilities from other Government agencies and shall

comply with the policies and procedures at [17.502-2](/part-17/17.502-2) , The Economy Act.
