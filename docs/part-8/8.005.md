# 8.005 Contract clause

Insert the clause at 52.208-9 , Contractor Use of Mandatory Sources of Supply and Services, in

solicitations  and contracts that require a contractor to provide supplies  or services for Government

use that are on the Procurement  List maintained by the Committee for Purchase From People Who

Are Blind or Severely Disabled. The contracting officer  shall  identify in the contract schedule the

supplies  or services that shall  be purchased from a mandatory source and the specific source.
