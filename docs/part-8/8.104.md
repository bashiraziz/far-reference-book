# 8.104 Obtaining nonreportable property

GSA will assist agencies in meeting their requirements for supplies  of the types excepted from

reporting as excess by the Federal Management Regulations (41 CFR 102-[36.220](/part-36/36.220)). Federal agencies

requiring such supplies  should  contact the appropriate GSA Personal Property  Management office.

Visit www.gsa.gov/ppmo  for contact information.
