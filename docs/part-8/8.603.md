# 8.603 Purchase priorities

FPI and nonprofit agencies participating in the AbilityOne Program under 41 U.S.C. chapter 85 ,

Committee for Purchase from People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled (see subpart  8.7 ) may

produce identical supplies  or services. When this occurs, ordering offices shall  purchase supplies

and services in the following priorities:

(a) Supplies .

(1) Federal Prison Industries, Inc. ( 41 U.S.C. 8504 ).

(2) AbilityOne participating nonprofit agencies.

(3) Commercial sources.

(b) Services.

(1) AbilityOne participating nonprofit agencies.

(2) Federal Prison Industries, Inc., or commercial sources.
