# 12.201 General

This subpart identifies special requirements for the acquisition  of commercial products  and

commercial services  intended to more closely resemble those customarily used in the commercial

marketplace, as well as other considerations necessary for proper planning, solicitation , evaluation,

and award of contracts for commercial products  and commercial services .
