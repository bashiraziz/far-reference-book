# 12.001 Definition

Subcontract , as used in this part, includes, but is not limited to, a transfer of commercial products  or

commercial services  between divisions, subsidiaries, or affiliates  of a contractor or subcontractor.

Subpart [12.1](/part-12/12.1)  - Acquisition of Commercial Products and

Commercial Services
