# 53.109 Forms prescribed by other regulations

Certain forms referred to in subpart  [53.2](/part-53/53.2)  are prescribed in other regulations and are specified by

the FAR for use in acquisition . For each of these forms, the prescribing agency is identified by

means of a parenthetical notation after the form number. For example, SF 1165  , which is

prescribed by the Government Accountability Office (GAO), is identified as SF 1165  (GAO).
