# 53.216-1 Delivery orders and orders under basic ordering agreements (OF 347)

OF 347  , Order for Supplies  or Services. OF 347  , prescribed in 53.213  (f) (or an approved agency

form), may be used to place orders under indefinite delivery contracts and basic ordering

agreements, as specified in 16.703  (d)(2)(i).
