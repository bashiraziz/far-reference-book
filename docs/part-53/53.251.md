# 53.251 Contractor use of Government supply sources (OF 347)

OF 347 , Order for Supplies  or Services . OF 347  , prescribed in [53.213](/part-53/53.213) (f), may be used by contractors

when requisitioning from the VA, as specified in [51.102](/part-51/51.102)  (e)(3)(ii).
