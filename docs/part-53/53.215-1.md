# 53.215-1 Solicitation and receipt of proposals

The following forms are prescribed, as stated in the following paragraphs, for use in contracting  by

negotiation (except for construction , architect-engineer services , or acquisitions  made using

simplified acquisition procedures ):

(a) SF 26  (Rev.3/2013), Award/Contract.  SF 26 , prescribed in [53.214](/part-53/53.214) (a), may be used in entering

into negotiated contracts in which the signature  of both parties on a single document is appropriate,

as specified in [15.509](/part-15/15.509) . Block 18 may not be used for negotiated procurements .
