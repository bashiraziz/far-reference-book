# 53.242-1 Novation and change-of-name agreements (SF 30)

SF 30 , Amendment of Solicitation /Modification of Contract . SF 30  , prescribed in [53.243](/part-53/53.243)  , shall  be

used in connection with novation and change of name agreements, as specified in 42.1203  (h).
