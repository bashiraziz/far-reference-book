# 46.303 Cost-reimbursement supply contracts

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at 52.246-3 , Inspection  of Supplies -Cost-

Reimbursement, in solicitations  and contracts for supplies , or services that involve the furnishing of

supplies , when a cost-reimbursement contract is contemplated.
