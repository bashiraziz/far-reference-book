# 46.304 Fixed-price service contracts

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.246-4](/part-52/52.246-4) , Inspection  of Services-Fixed-Price, in

solicitations  and contracts for services, or supplies  that involve the furnishing of services, when a

fixed-price contract is contemplated and the contract amount is expected to exceed the simplified

acquisition threshold . The contracting officer  may insert the clause in such solicitations  and

contracts when the contract amount is expected to be at or below the simplified acquisition

threshold  and inclusion is in the Governmentâ€™s interest.
