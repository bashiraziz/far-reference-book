# 46.601 General

Agencies shall  prescribe procedures and instructions for the use, preparation, and distribution of

material inspection  and receiving reports  and commercial shipping document/packing lists to

evidence Government inspection  (see [46.401](/part-46/46.401) ) and acceptance (see [46.501](/part-46/46.501) ).
