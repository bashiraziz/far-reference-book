# 46.312 Construction contracts

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at 52.246-12 , Inspection  of Construction , in

solicitations  and contracts for construction  when a fixed-price contract is contemplated and the

contract amount is expected to exceed the simplified acquisition threshold . The contracting officer

may insert the clause in such solicitations  and contracts when the contract amount is expected to be

at or below the simplified acquisition threshold , and its use is in the Governmentâ€™s interest.
