# 18.121 Bid guarantees

The chief of the contracting office  may waive the requirement to obtain a bid guarantee for

emergency  acquisitions  when a performance bond or a performance bond and payment bond is

required. (See [28.101-1](/part-28/28.101-1) (c).)
