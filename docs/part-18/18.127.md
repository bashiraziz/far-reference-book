# 18.127 Extraordinary contractual actions

subpart  [50.1](/part-50/50.1)  prescribes policies and procedures for entering into, amending, or modifying contracts

in order to facilitate the national defense  under the extraordinary emergency  authority granted by

Public Law 85-804 ( 50 U.S.C. 1431 -1434 ). This includes-

(a) Amending contracts without consideration (see [50.103-2](/part-50/50.103-2) (a));

(b) Correcting or mitigating mistakes in a contract (see [50.103-2](/part-50/50.103-2) (b)); and

(c) Formalizing informal commitments (See [50.103-2](/part-50/50.103-2) (c)).
