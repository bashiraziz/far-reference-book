# 18.114 Contracting with the Small Business Administration (The 8(a)

Program).

Contracts may be awarded to the Small Business Administration (SBA) for performance by eligible

8(a) participants on either a sole source or competitive basis. (See subpart  [19.8](/part-19/19.8) .)
