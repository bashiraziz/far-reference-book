# 36.602-4 Selection authority

(a) The final selection decision shall  be made by the agency head  or a designated selection authority.

(b) The selection authority shall  review the recommendations of the evaluation board and shall , with

the advice of appropriate technical and staff representatives, make the final selection. This final

selection shall  be a listing, in order of preference, of the firms considered most highly qualified to

perform the work. If the firm listed as the most preferred is not the firm recommended as the most

highly qualified by the evaluation board, the selection authority shall  provide for the contract file a

written explanation of the reason for the preference. All firms on the final selection list are
