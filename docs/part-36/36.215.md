# 36.215 Special procedure for cost-reimbursement contracts for construction

Contracting officers  may use a cost-reimbursement contract to acquire construction  only when its

use is consistent with subpart  [16.3](/part-16/16.3)  and part  15  (see [15.404-4](/part-15/15.404-4) (c)(4)(i) for fee limitation on cost-

reimbursement contracts).
