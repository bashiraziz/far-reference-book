# 36.001 Definitions

As used in this part-

Construction and demolition materials and debris  means waste materials and debris generated

during construction , renovation, demolition, or dismantling of all structures and buildings and

associated infrastructure.

Diverting  means redirecting materials from disposal in landfills or incinerators to recycling or

recovery, excluding diversion to waste-to-energy facilities.

Modernization project  means a project that includes the comprehensive replacement or restoration

of virtually all major systems , interior finishes (such as ceilings, partitions, doors, and floor finishes),

and building features.
