# 36.507 Permits and responsibilities

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.236-7](/part-52/52.236-7) , Permits and Responsibilities, in

solicitations  and contracts when a fixed-price or cost-reimbursement construction  contract or a

fixed-price dismantling, demolition, or removal of improvements contract is contemplated.
