# 36.523 Site visit

The contracting officer  shall  insert a provision substantially the same as the provision at 52.236-27 ,

Site Visit ( Construction ), in solicitations  which include the clauses at 52.236-2 , Differing Site

Conditions, and 52.236-3 , Site Investigations and Conditions Affecting the Work. AlternateI may be

used when an organized site visit will be conducted.
