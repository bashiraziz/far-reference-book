# 36.601-1 Public announcement

The Government shall  publicly announce all requirements for architect-engineer services  and

negotiate contracts for these services based on the demonstrated competence and qualifications of

prospective contractors to perform the services at fair and reasonable prices. (See 40 U.S.C. 1101  et

seq.)
