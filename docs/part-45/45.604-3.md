# 45.604-3 Proceeds from sales of surplus property

Proceeds of any sale are to be credited to the Treasury of the United States  as miscellaneous

receipts, unless otherwise authorized by statute or the contract or any subcontract thereunder

authorizes the proceeds to be credited to the price or cost of the work ( 40 U.S.C. 571  and 574).
