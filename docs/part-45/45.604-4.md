# 45.604-4 Sale of property pursuant to the exchange/sale authority

Agencies should  consider the sale of property pursuant to the exchange/sale authority in FMR

102-39 (41 CFRpart102-39) when agencies are acquiring or plan to acquire similar products  and

other requirements of the authority are satisfied.
