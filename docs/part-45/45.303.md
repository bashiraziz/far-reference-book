# 45.303 Use of Government property on independent research and

development programs.

The contracting officer  may authorize a contractor to use the property on an independent research

and development (IR&D) program, if-

(a) Such use will not conflict with the primary use of the property or enable the contractor to retain

property that could otherwise be released;

(b) The contractor agrees not to claim  reimbursement against any Government contract for the

rental value of the property; and

(c) A rental charge for the portion of the contractor’s IR&D program cost allocated to commercial

work is deducted from the claim  for reimbursement of any agreed-upon Government share of the

contractor’s IR&D costs.
