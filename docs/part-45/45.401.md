# 45.401 Title to Government-furnished property

The Government retains title to all Government-furnished property until properly disposed of, as

authorized by law or regulation. Property that is leased by the Government and subsequently

furnished to the contractor for use shall  be considered Government-furnished property under the

clause 52.245-1 , Government Property.
