# 22.504 General requirements for project labor agreements

(a) General . Project labor agreements  established under this subpart shall  fully conform to all

statutes, regulations, and Executive orders.

(b) Requirements . A project labor agreement  shall -

(1) Bind all contractors and subcontractors engaged in construction  on the construction  project to

comply with the project labor agreement ;

(2) Allow all contractors and subcontractors to compete for contracts and subcontracts without

regard to whether they are otherwise parties to collective bargaining agreements;

(3) Contain guarantees against strikes, lockouts, and similar job disruptions;

(4) Set forth effective, prompt, and mutually binding procedures for resolving labor disputes arising

during the term of the project labor agreement ;

(5) Provide other mechanisms for labor-management cooperation on matters of mutual interest and

concern, including productivity, quality of work, safety, and health; and

(6) Include any additional requirements as the agency deems necessary to satisfy its needs.

(c) Labor organizations . An agency may not require contractors or subcontractors to enter into a

project labor agreement  with any particular labor organization .
