# 22.610 Contract clause

The contracting officer  shall  insert the clause at [52.222-20](/part-52/52.222-20) , Contracts for Materials, Supplies ,

Articles, and Equipment, in solicitations  and contracts covered by the statute (see [22.603](/part-22/22.603) , [22.604](/part-22/22.604) ,

and [22.605](/part-22/22.605) ).
