# 22.404-11 Wage determination appeals

The Secretary of Labor has established an Administrative Review Board which decides appeals of

final decisions made by the Department of Labor concerning Construction  Wage Rate Requirements

statute wage determinations. A contracting  agency or other interested party may file a petition for

review under the procedures in 29 CFR Part 7  if reconsideration by the Administrator  has been

sought pursuant to 29 CFR1.8 and denied.
