# 53.109 Forms prescribed by other regulations.

Certain forms referred to in are prescribed in other regulations and are specified by the FAR for use in acquisition. For each of these forms, the prescribing agency is identified by means of a parenthetical notation after the form number. For example, , which is prescribed by the Government Accountability Office (GAO), is identified as (GAO).
