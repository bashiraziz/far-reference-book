# 53.212 Acquisition of commercial products and commercial services.

SF 1449 (Rev. Nov 2021), Solicitation/Contract/Order for Commercial Products and Commercial Services . is prescribed for use in solicitations and contracts for commercial products and commercial services. Agencies may prescribe additional detailed instructions for use of the form.
